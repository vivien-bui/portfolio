# Content Operations

## Content Types

| Type | What it means |
|------|--------------|
| **Net New** | Original content created from scratch for SharkBeauty AU/NZ |
| **Remix** | Adapted from existing global or other-market assets |
| **Handle** | Reposted or reshared from another account or creator |

---

## Posting Rules

### ⚠️ 2-Hour Gap Rule
There must be a **minimum 2-hour gap** between posts across all handles.
This is a **hard rule** — no back-to-back scheduling. Ever.

### ✅ Upload to Consolidated Deck Before Posting
**Content must be uploaded within the consolidated deck before it is posted.**
This is the approval gate. Do not skip this step unless it is a genuine emergency/high-priority situation — and even then, flag it.

> Skipping the deck upload is the #1 process error on this account.

# Watch Outs

> These are the things that will catch you out if you're not paying attention.

*[Place Image 10 here — watch outs callout visual]*

---

### 1. 2-Hour Posting Gap
Hard rule. No exceptions without explicit client sign-off. Set your scheduler, then manually check times before confirming.

### 2. Upload to Consolidated Deck Before Posting
The deck upload is the approval gate. Content goes in the deck **before** it goes live. If you're in doubt about whether something has been approved, it hasn't.

### 3. Dash Select All Bug
Select All in Dash is unreliable. Always verify manually before bulk actions.

### 4. AU vs NZ Are Separate
Different handles, different calendars, different audiences. Never assume an AU post automatically applies to NZ or vice versa.
