## 5. AIM Framework

AIM = **Analyse → Ideate → Make**

This is the core strategic operating rhythm for the account.

| Stage | What happens | PCS contribution |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|

## 7. Content Operations

### Content Types

| Type | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Net New** | Original content created from scratch for SharkBeauty AU/NZ |
| **Remix** | Adapted from existing global/other-market assets |
| **Handle** | Content reposted or reshared from another account or creator |

### Posting Rules

- **2-hour gap rule:** There must be a minimum 2-hour gap between posts across all handles. This is a hard rule — do not schedule posts back-to-back.
- **Content must be uploaded within the consolidated deck before posting** unless in an extreme/high-priority circumstance. This is critical — do not skip this step.
